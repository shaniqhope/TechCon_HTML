History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into one of the leading technology conferences in the world, bringing together thought leaders and experts from various industries. The event is known for its inspiring keynotes and innovative exhibits that showcase the future of technology.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we believe in the power of technology to change the world. Our mission is to provide a platform where innovators, thought leaders, and tech professionals can connect, learn, and share groundbreaking ideas that will shape the future of the industry. TechCon aims to promote innovation, collaboration, and inclusion in the technology sector.

Notable Past Speakers

Elon Musk

Elon Musk is the CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, known for his groundbreaking work in space exploration and electric vehicles. At TechCon 2020, Elon shared insights on the future of space travel and his vision for a multi-planetary society.

Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Google and Alphabet. At TechCon 2018, Sundar discussed the role of AI in shaping the future of technology and how Google is using AI to make information accessible to everyone.

Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg is the COO of Meta (formerly Facebook) and author of the best-selling book "Lean In." In her keynote at TechCon 2019, Sheryl spoke about leadership, innovation, and the importance of diversity in technology.
